How it works

A full course in four parts.

Each subject is broken down into topics, lessons, practice, and tests — the same structure your school uses, just always available.

1

Watch a lesson

Short, focused video lessons taught by experienced teachers. Pause, rewind, replay — at your own pace.

2

Read the notes

Clear, written summaries for every topic. The kind of notes you'd borrow from a friend if you had a good one.

3

Practise questions

Worked examples, then practice questions that get harder as you go. Instant feedback, no waiting for a teacher.

4

Test yourself

End-of-topic tests and timed past-paper questions. Track what you've nailed and what to go back to.

Inside a subject

Every topic. Every chapter. In order.

Open a subject and you'll see the full syllabus, broken into chapters and lessons. Pick up wherever you left off, or jump to the topic your teacher is on this week.
